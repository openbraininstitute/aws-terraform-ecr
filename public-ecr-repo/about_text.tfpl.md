## What is the Open Brain Institute?

The [Open Brain Institute](https://www.openbraininstitute.org/) provides a platform which allows neuroscience enthusiasts to launch virtual lab to explore, build, and experiment with digital brain models.
It is based on the scientific legacy of the Blue Brain Project, which created millions of neuronal morphologies, electrophysiological recordings, and meticulously detailed digital brain models across multiple scales.
The [Open Brain Institute](https://www.openbraininstitute.org/) platform simplifies the access to the powerful open-source tools to build, simulate, analyze, and visualize biologically accurate digital brain models.
